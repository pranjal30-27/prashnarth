About Prashnarth

Prashnarth is an interactive learning platform designed to help students, developers, and knowledge seekers explore a wide range of subjects in a simple and engaging way. Our goal is to provide questions, answers, and resources that make learning easier and fun.

Our Mission

To create a space where anyone can find structured questions with clear answers, practice coding, and build their knowledge step by step. We aim to make learning accessible, effective, and enjoyable.

What We Offer

  • Topic-wise categorized questions and answers
  • Multiple programming language support
  • Copy-answer and save-for-later features
  • Simple, responsive, and user-friendly design

Why Choose Us?

Unlike generic platforms, Prashnarth focuses on simplicity and clarity. We don’t just give answers—we help you understand the logic behind them. Whether you are preparing for exams, interviews, or personal growth, Prashnarth is your perfect companion.
